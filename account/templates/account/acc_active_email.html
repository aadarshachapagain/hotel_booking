Welcome To BIG SAFAR

Big Safar is an online travel service, first of its kind in Nepal offering a variety of services to its users. Big Safar's mobile application is a one-stop shop for any kind of travel related experience aimed at delivering a complete positive travel experience.

SERVICES AND PRODUCT

Services and products provided by Big Safar will initially include pre-arranged tours, customized tours, hotel booking, restaurants and vehicle rental service. Big Safar seeks to differentiate itself as the first online travel service provider in Nepal. Our services will be positioned carefully: they will be of extremely good quality, comfortable, informative and tailored to the client needs such that they will enable individuals to have a great appreciation of the natural environment and its intricacies.

YOUR PEACE OF MIND IS OUR TOP PRIORITY

Here’s how we’re helping you feel confident welcoming guests:

  • Your place, your rules – Pick your prices, policies, and rules for guests.
  • We’ve got your back – Access to 24/7 support and guest misconduct reporting.
  • Find your perfect match – Set requirements and criteria guests need to fulfill.

WORKING WITH US

  • Open and close your property when you want
    We give you freedom to open or close your property on your site whenever you want. With no lock-in contracts, you are in control of your property.
  • We don’t charge guests
    We don’t add anything on to the price. Unlike other platforms that usually charge guests, the price you set is the price we display.
  • Using Big Safar alongside other platforms
    Using Big Safar along with your other relationships isn’t a problem. With our simple calendar, you can manage your availability parallel to your existing relationship.

PRICING AND COMMISSION

  • Your price your control:
    Our pricing structure is based on the idea that price you set is what guests pay. We don’t add anything on top-this way; you can price your property competitively and welcome more guests.
  • Commission Percentage10 % of the Selling Price excluding Tax that you set for customers.
