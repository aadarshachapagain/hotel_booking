PRIVACY STATEMENT FOR BUSINESS PARTNERS


This Privacy Statement applies to Ktm Voyage. It describes how Ktm Voyage collects and processes personal data about (potential, existing or former) Trip Providers and other business partners. This Privacy Statement therefore applies to personal data of owners, employees, representatives or other individuals acting on behalf of a Trip Provider or other business partners (Trip Providers, other business partners and the related individuals are hereafter together or individually referred to as "Business Partners" or "you"). A Business Partner within the meaning of this Privacy Statement may be a Trip Provider (including the provider of accommodation, attractions, park, museum, etc.), business partners enabling the reservations of Trips through their websites and/or apps or other means, including travel agencies, third party vendors and other business partners maintaining a business relationship with Ktm Voyage.


Data Collection


The personal data Ktm Voyage collects in regards to Business Partners depends on the context of the business relationship and interaction with Ktm Voyage, the choices made by a Business Partner and the products, services and features used. The personal data Ktm Voyage collects about a Business Partner can include the following - depending on the specific situation, not all of the following data items may be considered personal data because they may relate to a business instead of an individual:

Personal data you give to us


Contact details:
We collect contact data of Business Partner, such as first name/ last name, date of birth (as required), business e-mail address and address, telephone and fax number.

Financial data:
We collect data necessary for payment, including invoicing purposes, including your bank details: bank account number, VAT number, credit slips.

Verification details:
As part of the registration process, Ktm Voyage can ask (representatives of) Business Partners to provide a copy of their ID card or passport, a photo, video or other relevant information to verify the authenticity of the Business Partner.

Others:
When communicating with Ktm Voyage, Ktm Voyage collects and processes communications. During calls with our support center, live listening may be done or calls may be recorded for quality control and training purposes, which includes the usage of the recordings for the handling of claims and fraud detection purposes. Recordings are kept for a limited amount of time and automatically deleted, unless Ktm Voyage has a legitimate interest to keep such recording for a longer period, including for fraud investigation and legal purposes.

Other information we collect automatically:
When using the Ktm Voyage online services, such as the online registration form, an online user account (e.g. partner center), or make use of a Ktm Voyage app, Ktm Voyage also collects information automatically, some of which may be personal data. This includes data such as language settings, IP address, location, device settings, device OS, log information, time of usage, URL requested, status report, user agent (information about the browser version), operating system, result (viewer or booker), browsing history, user Booking ID, and type of data viewed. By sharing other individual’s personal data in relation to your business (for example of your staff members), you confirm that these individuals have been informed about the use of their personal data by Ktm Voyage in accordance with this Privacy Statement and provided any necessary consents.

Other information we receive from other sources:


Information on insolvencies:
In insolvency matters, Ktm Voyage can receive information from insolvency administrators, courts or other public authorities relating to a Business Partner.

Data related to law enforcement and tax authority requests:
Law enforcement or tax authorities can contact Ktm Voyage with additional information about Business Partners in case they are affected by an investigation.

Fraud detection:
In certain instances, and as permitted by applicable law, Ktm Voyage may need to collect data through third party sources for fraud detection and prevention purposes. Ktm Voyage uses account details, financial data and contact details for registrations with Ktm Voyage, including verification purposes to manage the business relationship with the Business Partner.
